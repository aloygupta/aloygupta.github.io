Poet With A Gun



I wanted to be a poet for long,
Wanted to write some stuff very strong.
Attacking every idea out there,
Every ideology and every dogma here.

Some people want it suppressed,
They would rather be opressed,
Some are indoctrinated,
With ideas dated and ill fated.

And, they want to beat me up,
Silence me and lock me up,
Threatening me to not write down,
What I feel and leave the town.

But, speech should always remain free,
Like the burning sun and deep blue sea.
To defend freedom and not be done,
I bought a pen and bought a gun.
