Sunglasses



The afternoon is still bright,
The sun dazzles in all its might.
In a pair of sunglasses, I have sought refuge,
I stepped into a sea of crowd, such huge.

Although I can be seen by most,
I can still pretend to be a ghost,
With hidden eyes, with a sly smile,
An air of nonchalance, that's the style.

Trying to run away fast and hide,
Every version of myself has died,
The only self that I know,
Is the one that I don't know.

The evenings are breezy and cool,
I jumped out like a fool,
No sunglasses to hide behind,
I had to make up my mind.

A thousand versions of me,
I had to choose one to be,
No more I chose any other self,
Finally, I gladly chose myself.
