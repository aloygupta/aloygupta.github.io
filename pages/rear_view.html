Rear View



As the wind blows in my hair,
The thrill and chill in the air,
A long drive I have embarked on,
A short one, others have warned.

It's been a smooth drive until now,
But, the highway road is getting rough, anyhow.
A car from a distant past has turned up,
I looked at the rear view for a close up.

It's honking and threatening to overtake,
For a second, I almost pressed my brake.
But, this is a car I know well,
Shall I get into a fist fight, shall I yell?

It's a car from my distant past,
Had troubled me a lot and is driving fast.
But, I am calmer than I have ever been,
Wearing a smile no one has never seen.

I have my hands on the wheel,
Detached and numb is what I feel.
I pressed the accelerator and zoomed ahead,
The car in my rear view, started to fade.
