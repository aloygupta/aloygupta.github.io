Slow Burn

Well, I like it slow,

Take it easy with the flow,

The world is uncontrollable,

Unpredictable and uncomfortable

To be taken too seriously,

And we ain't getting out alive.

...

Walk slowly while the world keeps running,

Talk with ease while the world keeps hustling,

And you can see how the noise drowns us out,

Makes you unheard and inflicts self doubt.

...

You can fight modernity by giving up on it,

Letting life, like a thriller, burn slow.

Not clinging to instant dopamine shots,

But, making it rare, and letting yourself go.
